What We Do

Cutting Edge Research

InCred Equities provides cutting-edge equities research and trading services to clients across Institutions/Corporates, Ultra-High/High-Net-Worth Individuals and Small & Medium Enterprises.

We are part of InCred Group of companies, which is one of India’s fast-growing diversified financial service conglomerates with business across Wealth Management, Asset Management, Investment Banking, Institutional Equities Research & Broking and Retail & MSME lending.

Led by thought-provoking research, InCred’s Institutional Equities has quickly grown into one of India’s most respected and fastest-growing Institutional Equities franchises. Over the years, our teams have been voted top-tier in various sectors by institutional investors. With an on-ground presence across India, Singapore and the US is built on a base of over 150 clients.

Our strategic networks transcend borders - with a strong combination and collaboration between local offices, branches, and partners, along with CGS-CIMB global offices in London, New York, and 7 Asian countries, we promise our clients a truly global presence.

We are a fast-growing financial services firm known for having a strong focus on in-depth research and analysis of financial products.
