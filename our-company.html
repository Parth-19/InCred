Who We Are

Who Are We?

InCred Research is a part of InCred Group.

InCred is one of India's premier providers of financial advice and capital, which strongly focuses on delivering tailor-made financial solutions that are suitable to all our client's needs. These are based on our deep understanding of the Indian economy and market forces, unmatched research and our client-focused approach.

Our strong business acumen gives us the ability to see the big picture and understand the levers that affect the business drivers through smart decisions and actions that can increase the long-term profitability for our clients and ourselves resulting in sustainable growth. At InCred, we possess the ability to break down complexity while suggesting the finest financial solutions to our clients.

Experience

With our founding and the senior executive team bringing strong global experience, our entrenched international network through CGS-CIMB and award-winning research specialists, we bring a great blend of both global expertise and domestic coverage across 20 sectors and 195 companies to successfully deliver value to our clients.

Expertise

A team that is a powerful combination of experience and knowledge forms the core of our offerings. This, combined with robust technology, gives us the ability to infuse speed and flexibility in decision-making and deliver effectively.



Technology

At InCred, we believe that technology is a powerful tool and leverage it efficiently to enhance our quality of work. Our advanced Algorithmic Trading techniques for our institutional clients, mixed with state-of-the-art Direct Market Access (DMS) systems, offer a unique blend of services.



Industrial Knowledge

Our Equity Capital Market teams have successfully raised equity for several clients across a plethora of sectors, including Infrastructure, Logistics, Consumer, Entertainment, Banking, Financial Services, Pharmaceuticals, Real Estate, Ports, Telecom Infrastructure, Information Technology, Aviation, Cement, Oil & Gas lending us the capability to provide superior and agile services.
